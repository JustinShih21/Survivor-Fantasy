# Research Sources for Simulation Probabilities

This document cites the sources used to calibrate the Survivor fantasy point simulation.

## Idol Statistics

- **Idols found per season:** 2-4 typical in modern era (S33+). Rick Devens (S38) found 4; Tai (S34) and Ben (S35) found 3. Source: True Dork Times, Survivor Wiki.
- **Idol play success rate:** ~67% when played at tribal (40 successful vs 20 wasted through S35). Source: [Reality Blurred - 137 Survivor votes cancelled](https://www.realityblurred.com/realitytv/2018/01/survivor-hidden-immunity-idol-data/).
- **Voted out with idol in pocket:** ~17% of voted-out contestants had an idol they never played. Source: Reality Blurred idol data analysis.
- **Idol never played:** ~13% of idols found are never played. Source: Survivor idol play data (Google Sheets compilation).

## Advantages

- **Advantages vs idols:** Idols comprise ~80% of "advantages" in play; 169 total advantages across 42 seasons. Source: Gradient Descending Survivor advantages dataset.
- **Non-idol advantages:** Steal a vote, Knowledge is Power, etc. 0-1 successful plays per season typical. Source: Survivor Wiki, season recaps.

## Vote Counts

- **Pre-merge:** 4-6 votes typical (tribe of 6).
- **Post-merge:** 5-9 votes typical (merged tribe of 10-12).
- Source: Typical Survivor tribal council structure.

## Clue Reads

- **Clues per season:** 0-2 separate clue-read events (clues often lead to idol finds).
- Source: Survivor format conventions.

## Season Structure

- **Episodes:** 13-14 regular + finale. 13 tribals with votes.
- **Merge:** Typically episode 8-9, ~12 people remaining.
- **Cast:** 18 contestants, 3 tribes of 6.
- Source: Survivor Wiki, typical season format.
